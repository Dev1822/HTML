New Education Policy Announced

The government has introduced significant changes in the national curriculum aimed at modernizing the education system and making it more skill-oriented. The new policy emphasizes practical learning, critical thinking, and the integration of technology into classrooms. It also proposes reducing rote memorization and increasing flexibility in subject choices for students. Experts believe these reforms will better prepare young people for higher education and the rapidly evolving job market. Schools and universities across the country are expected to begin implementing the changes over the next academic year.


Floods in Assam

Heavy rains over the past several days have caused massive floods in Assam, displacing thousands of residents and damaging homes, crops, and infrastructure. Several rivers in the region have overflowed their banks, leading to road closures and power outages in multiple districts. Rescue teams and the National Disaster Response Force have been deployed to provide relief, distribute essential supplies, and evacuate affected families to safer locations. Authorities are urging residents in low-lying areas to remain alert as more rainfall is forecast in the coming days.
